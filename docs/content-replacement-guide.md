# Content replacement guide

Before publication, replace and approve:

- Sutur wordmark/icon and Official Odoo Partner asset in `public/brand/`.
- Licensed Gotham web fonts, or approve the current fallback.
- Founder/team names, roles, bios, photos, credentials, and profile links.
- Business contact email, privacy policy, and terms.
- Booking recipient email, sender domain/provider, and acknowledgement wording.
- Public domain, metadata, social image, and analytics/conversion events.
- Google Calendar credentials and the manual approval operating policy.

Do not publish placeholders or add testimonials, metrics, customer names, or credentials without written approval.
